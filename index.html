Welcome to Robocup Simulator.

This simulator was developed to try out various ideas and algorithms for the Robotex 2012 competition. The event was held on 25. november where the robot got the second place in the very competitive professional robotic football league.

The simulator is configured to control a robot with four omni-wheels and two cameras but other arrangements can be created. Click on the screenshot below to have a go against the simple AI using keyboard WASDQE buttons or a gamepad.
